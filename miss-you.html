I Really Miss You

It's strange how silence feels louder when you're not here. Every little thing reminds me of you.

My love, every day without you feels incomplete. The world continues moving, but something inside me pauses, waiting for your voice, your smile, your presence.

I miss the way you laugh at the smallest things, the way your eyes light up when you talk about your dreams. Even the quiet moments with you meant everything to me.

Nights feel longer now. I look at the stars and wonder if you're looking at the same sky. Somehow that thought makes me feel closer to you.

If I could hold your hand right now, I would never let it go so easily again. You are not just someone I love — you are the part of my heart that feels like home.

Until we meet again, know that I carry you in every heartbeat, in every breath, and in every silent prayer.
